Selecting temporary and permanent guardians and trustees for minor age or special needs children

There will be times when you will travel out of town and choose to leave your children at home and in the care of a family member or close friend. Have you provided them with the temporary legal authority enabling them to approve medical treatment should your child become ill or injured in your absence?

When you and your spouse pass-on, who should be the guardian to care for your minor age children? Who should be your personal representative to identify your assets, pay any debts, and distribute your net estate? Who should be the trustee to administer and distribute your children’s inheritance until they are old enough to manage it for themselves?
